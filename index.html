Simple Cross-Device Sharing

How to Share Across Devices (Guaranteed to Work!):

Step 1: Export

Click "Export Data" to copy all your tasks to clipboard

Step 2: Share

Send the exported text via WhatsApp, email, or text message

Step 3: Import

On another device, paste the text and click "Import Data"

Step 4: Done!

All tasks, employees, and progress are now synced!

Works On Any Device:

  • iPhone/Android: Copy and paste via messaging apps
  • Tablets: Share via email or cloud notes
  • Computers: Save to text files or send via email
  • No Internet Required: Works completely offline
  • 100% Private: Your data never leaves your devices
